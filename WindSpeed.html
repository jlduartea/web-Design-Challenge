Wind Speed














According to the analysis carried out, we can confirm that the wind speed is higher the farther from the equator.

However, there is not enough data for cities that are close to the North and South Poles, so it is only possible to conclude that there is a relationship between latitude and wind speed, without being able to qualify it in terms of correlation.
